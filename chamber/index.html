    Our History

    Mdantsane, the second-largest township in South Africa, is not just a home to vibrant culture and resilient people—it's a growing hub of entrepreneurial energy. Founded in the 1960s, Mdantsane has transformed into a thriving urban township with diverse industries.

    The Mdantsane Chamber of Commerce was established to nurture this spirit. We work to support local businesses, drive innovation, and connect entrepreneurs with opportunities. Today, we’re proud to be a voice for over 300 local businesses and counting.
