Our Story

Township Event Rentals was founded in 2022 in Mdantsane with a mission to provide affordable, reliable event equipment for all types of township events. From small family gatherings to large-scale community celebrations, we’ve helped hundreds of residents bring their events to life.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant township community where every resident has access to the tools and support they need to host meaningful, memorable events. Our goal is to be the leading event rental service provider across East London and beyond — building unity through service, affordability, and community focus.

Community Support & Giving Back

We actively support local schools, churches, and youth initiatives through equipment donations and discounted services. 10% of all December bookings go toward sponsoring holiday events for underprivileged families. If you’d like to contribute, please click here to donate or offer sponsorships.
